Experiment 10: Autoencoder Image Denoising
Dataset: MNIST IDX files

MODEL:
  - Type: Convolutional Autoencoder
  - Input shape: 28x28x1
  - Loss: Mean Squared Error
  - Optimizer: Adam (lr=0.001)
  - Epochs max: 2
  - Batch size: 128
  - Noise factor: 0.4

RESULTS:
  - Baseline MSE (Noisy vs Clean): 0.079675
  - Denoised MSE (Autoencoder): 0.103979
  - PSNR Noisy: 10.998 dB
  - PSNR Denoised: 10.145 dB
